How to Register with Detective Pikachu

Send a private message to @DetectivePikachuBot

- iPhone: click @DetectivePikachuBot, then Send Message

- Android: click @DetectivePikachuBot


In PM with @DetectivePikachuBot, click Start

- Send /register followed by your in-game name when asked e.g. 

/register PokemonTrainer1

- The bot will ask you to nickname a particular PokÃ©mon and set it as your buddy, i.e Bulbasaur named Olive

- Follow the instructions carefully and take a screenshot of your Trainer profile (careful not to change the position of your trainer) and send this to the bot.

- Wait for a success message to appear.

- Finally, send your trainer code so that it can be added to your profile.

If you need to check your details, send /profile to @DetectivePikachuBot to find out what is currently registered.

To change your team or level in future simply send another screenshot to the bot and it will update automatically.

Note: that @DetectivePikachuBot is only active in this group and cannot read your private messages or access any other groups / channels.
