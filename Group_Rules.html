Raiding:

We aim to keep things as fair as possible to give everyone the best chance so please make sure to obey the following rules:

  1. Use the raid chat and DetectivePikachu bot to create, join and keep track of all raids that you're interested in (especially T4 and T5). All T4/T5 raids attended by members must be posted to the group so everyone has the chance to join; please don't be selfish.
  2. Be punctual, once a raid meet time is set and agreed upon it cannot be changed unless all participants agree (some players may have other commitments i.e. work that they need to get back to).
  3. When setting a raid time leave at least 15 minutes between the meet time and the time that the raid boss disappears to allow for any issues for example; GPS drift / network connectivity.
  4. Issue a 10 minute warning at the time shown in bold by DetectivePikachu (this is the meet time) before starting. This is required for all major (T4/T5) raids.
  5. Start only when the 10 minute warning has elapsed. This ensures all group members are clear about timings, including those driving.
  6. Use a private group for T4 and T5 raids to prevent cheaters from joining the lobby and benefitting. Look around for any people playing to invite them to join (regardless of whether or not they are group members).
  7. Split larger groups (15+) into teams of Instinct, Mystic & Valor to maximise chances and in-game bonuses i.e. extra balls. If this is not possible then split the group into equal numbers with a balance of over and under level 30.
